Privacy Policy

Like millions of other websites, we use Google Analytics here on www.team5926.org

Google Analytics is a piece of software that grabs data about our visitors (you). It works like an (really) advanced server log.

What does Google Analytics record?

  • What website you came from to get here.
  • What type of devices you’re using.
  • And quite a bit more.

What do we do with all that data?

The tracking information allows us to better understand the kind of people who come to our website and what content they are looking at. This helps us to make better decisions about design and website content.

All of our activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service.

Do you want to opt out of tracking?

You can opt out of Google’s advertising tracking cookie or use a browser plugin to opt out of all Google Analytics tracking software.
