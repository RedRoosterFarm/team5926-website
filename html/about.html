About Us

We are Da MOOse, a small rookie 4-H FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team. Our goal for our first season was to learn how to build a robot and create a robot that could compete. We succeded by learning how to build and program our robot, gain knowledge about science and technology and most important of all, collaborate as a team to build a robot that accomplished the goal of the game and we surpassed our goal by winning two district competitions, qualified for states and then worlds.

We competed in our first district event at Kettering University on March 11th & 12th, 2016. We finished ranked 10th, through the alliance selection process we moved up to become the 6th place alliance captain. From there we went on to win it all and take home the blue banner. UNBELIEVABLE!!

    Who We Are


    We are Da MOOse, a small second year FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team. Our goal for our first season was to learn how to build a robot and create a robot that could compete. We succeeded by learning how to build and program our robot, gain knowledge about science and technology and most important of all, collaborate as a team to build a robot that accomplished the goal of the game. We surpassed our goal by winning two district competitions, qualified for the 2016 Michigan State Championship and then advanced to the 2016 World Championship.
