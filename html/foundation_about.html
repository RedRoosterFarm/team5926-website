We are Da MOOse, a small veteran FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team. Our goal for our first season was to learn how to build a robot and create a robot that could compete. We succeded by learning how to build and program our robot, gain knowledge about science and technology and most important of all, collaborate as a team to build a robot that accomplished the goal of the game and we surpassed our goal by winning two district competitions, qualified for states and then worlds.
