Da MOOse (a.k.a. MooBotics) in 2016


Originally FRC Team 5926 was affiliated with 4-H and had chosen the name MooBotics to reflect the organization’s ties to agriculture. The team is now affiliated with a different non-profit, so it was decided to change the logo and brand identity. Not an easy task, as the MooBotics logo and brand had become internationally recognized because of the team’s outstanding rookie season.

MooBotics competed in its first district event at Kettering University on March 11th & 12th, 2016. The team finished the qualification matches and ranked 10th. Through the alliance selection process Moobotics moved up to become the 6th place alliance captain. From there, they went on to win it all and take home the blue banner. UNBELIEVABLE!!
